# Roles and permissions

Use the smallest role that lets someone do their job.

| Role | Use for |
| --- | --- |
| Admin | People who manage site settings, permissions, and publishing. |
| Creator | People who create spaces and larger content areas. |
| Editor | People who write and update pages. |
| Reviewer | People who approve change requests. |
| Commenter | People who give feedback without editing. |
| Reader | People who only need access to read. |

## Suggested test setup

For this sandbox, keep <code class="expression">space.vars.owner</code> as the main owner and grant admin access only to people helping with setup.
